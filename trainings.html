About Us
  • The United States clinical research industry market is estimated to reach about $22 billion by 2021. The employment rate in the industry has soared, growing rapidly above the growth rate of other industries. On the other hand there is a shortage of qualified clinical research professionals to effectively and efficiently manage the process. The Capital Staffing has a 100% success track record in industry job placement. We strive to ensure that students are properly trained to industry standard, thereby making the job placement process seamless. Our Job placement specialist will help our students with networking, developing application strategies and outreach to pharmaceutical and clinical research organizations to ensure they find meaningful employment in the field.
